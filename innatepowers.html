GAMEPLAY UPDATE
THE INNATE POWERS
Map Changes

The map is now smaller but about 10% bigger than the pre-7.33 map, with the majority of the extra space being around the Rosh Pits located at the top-left and bottom-right sides of the map. Compared to the 7.32 map, there are 2-2 more Neutral Camps on each side, both of which are located around the Rosh Pits.

Twin Gates were moved to the location where the Tormentors used to be. They do not require to be channeled anymore; the teleporting is instant; however, they place a dispellable debuff on the teleported units that silences and mutes them for 4 seconds.

Roshan pits are still located at the edges of the map; however, he does not use Twin Gates anymore to travel between them. Roshan now uses an underground cave system, which is inaccessible by heroes, to travel to the other pit. How he dug this cave system and gets to the other side so quickly will remain a trade secret.

Jungle layout has been completely reworked.

Economy

Passive gold per minute is now static and has increased from 90 to 100.Previously increased up to 150 per minute.

There is now an extra melee creep that spawns on both teams offlane.

Denied Creeps now give 25% of their gold bounty to the denier.

Jungle Creeps gold and experience rewards are now dynamic : Their bounties are 40% more at the start of the game, but they decrease by 10% every 5 minutes for 20 minutes. After that their bounties increase by 10% every 5 minutes for 20 minutes and repeat.

Runes

Bounty Runes reworked. They now spawn at minute 2, near the ancient camp, and continue to spawn every 2 minutes. Their bounty, which is 90 gold and experience, is no longer granted to the whole team; instead, it is given only to the hero that picks them up. This bounty scales as the game progresses, granting +15 gold and experience with each spawn.

Wisdom Runes have been removed.Experience is now granted by Bounty Runes.

Structures

Lotus Pools now generate Healing Lotuses every 2 minutes. They previously generated a Healing Lotus every 3 minutes.

Tower Protection aura is now only applied around tier 3/4 towers and only grants bonus armor now. Previously also granted +1/3 health regeneration.

Tower Protection aura bonus armor decreased from +3/+5/+5/+5 to +3.

Tier 2 towers do not have Split Shot ability anymore.

Tier 1 towers attack range are increased from 700 to 800.

Creeps and Roshan

Roshan now drops a Tome of Aghanim upon his third death on the radiant side instead of Aghanim's Blessing.New item. Grants the Aghanim's Scepter upgrade to the consumer for 5 minutes.

Flagbearer Creeps now only spawn on the mid lane and grant 175% of the experience of a melee creep instead of bonus gold.

Flagbearer Creep Inspirational Aura now gives +15% movement speed in a 600 radius instead of +3 bonus health regeneration.

Lane Creeps had their levels decreased :

Melee Creeps level are decreased from 3 to 2.

Ranged Creeps level are decreased from 5 to 4.

Lane Creeps do not move faster during night time anymore.

Ancient Creeps had their levels increased:

Small Ancient Creeps levels are increased from 3 to 10.

Big Ancient Creeps levels are increased from 6 to 12.

Ancient Creeps now have 100% magic resistance.

Attributes

Strength now grants 20 health per point.Previously gave 22 health per point.

Agility now grants 1 armor per 7 points.Previously gave 1 armor per 6 points.

Intelligence does not grant 0.1% magic resistance per point anymore.

Intelligence now grants 10 mana per point.Previously gave 12 mana per point.

States and Mechanics

Spell Immunity and Debuff Immunity have been reworked:

Spell Immunity now grants 100% spell damage resistance and makes units in this state untargetable by most spells.

Debuff Immunity now only grants complete resistance against negative effects, but the abilities still do full damage to units in this state, and previously acquired debuffs will not be dispelled, only temporarily ineffective.For example, Dual Breath does not slow debuff immune units but still does full damage.

Hex now has two versions:

Greater Hex. Transforms a unit into a sheep, removing its secondary attributes, for example, magic resistance, evasion, and spell lifesteal. This unit has 300 movement speed while transformed and its movement speed can be increased by movement speed boosts.

Lesser Hex. Transforms a unit into a frog, decreasing its movement speed to 100. This unit's movement speed cannot be increased while transformed.

New state: Frozen. Frozen units are forbidden from casting spells and attacking but can use items. Abilities that now use this mechanic are, for example, Crystal Maiden's Frostbite and Drow Ranger's Frost Arrows. This state is dispelled by any kind of forced movement, for example Force Staff's Force ability.

Units with the flying state now elevate from the ground by 190 units, therefore heroes with a shorter attack range than 190 cannot attack them anymore.

Break is now always dispellable and does not get applied on abilities that are in the ultimate ability slot.

Spell Lifesteal reduction on creeps decreased from 75% to 50%.

Hero Class Updates

New hero class



Maximum level has been decreased from level 30 to level 25.

Heroes do not move faster during night time anymore.

Heroes and their summons are now granted a bonus 25 movement speed buff if they are traversing through the enemy side of the map.

There are now class specific benefits that heroes gain depending on their primary attribute :

Strength heroes now gain 0.5 damage block per strength.The chance to block is 50% and works on ranged heroes too.

Agility heroes now gain 0.08% movement speed per agility.

Intelligence heroes now gain 0.06% cooldown reduction per intelligence.

New hero class type: Style. These heroes allow the players to build them however they want, depending on their playstyle, by having a fluid primary attribute. The primary attribute is determined by which attribute the hero has the most of. For example, a hero like Pangolier, who gains more agility than strength, can be turned into a strength hero by buying items that give strength.

Universal Hero pool reduced from 34 to 8. These heroes are now Void Spirit, Enigma, Faceless Void, Chaos Knight, Keeper of the Light, IO, Elder Titan, and Weaver.

Universal Heroes now fully benefit from all attribute bonuses such as damage block per strength, movement speed per agility, and cooldown reduction per intelligence.

Universal Hero damage gain per stat reduced from 0.7 to 0.5.

Heroes now have their own innate abilities. These abilities differ from being a useful tool early on to being a game-changing late-game ability to abuse. They also differ in leveling: some can be leveled, while others cannot. A few of these innate abilities will be familiar, for example, Invoker's Invoke or Earth Spirit's Stone Remnants; however, others, like Chaotic Luck or Black Grimoire, will probably not be.

Innate damage block for melee heroes is removed.

Heroes now have a base 20% spell damage resistance.Heroes previously had a base 25% magic resistance.
This means physical damage spells also do less damage to heroes now, for example, Boundless Strike.

Heroes do not get a free Teleport Scroll anymore upon death.

Damage Type Updates

Two new damage types



Pure Damage:

Does not get reduced by spell damage resistance or reductions but also does not benefit from spell amplification anymore.

Chaos Damage:

A new type of magic damage that ignores magic resistance, but gets reduced by spell damage reductions and resistance.
Dominated Damage:

Creeps set under control by Helm of The Dominator or a summon hero like Chen now gain this damage type. Units with this damage type deal 200% damage to heroes, 150% to creeps and 100% to structures.
