Base Game Updates
Removals
Tormentors are removed.

Watchers are removed.

Jungle Zones are removed.

Outposts are removed.

Neutral Items are removed

Talents are removed

Universal heroes are now reverted to their original primary attribute.
General mechanics
The map is now smaller but about 10% bigger than the pre-7.33 map, with the majority of the extra space being around the Rosh Pits located at the top-left and bottom-right sides of the map. Compared to the 7.32 map, there are 2-2 more Neutral Camps on each side, both of which are located around the Rosh Pits.

Twin Gates were moved to the location where the Tormentors used to be. They do not require to be channeled anymore; the teleporting is instant; however, they place a dispellable debuff on the teleported units that silences and mutes them for 4 seconds.

Roshan still has two pits located at the edges of the map; however, he does not use Twin Gates anymore to travel between them. Roshan now uses an underground cave system, which is inaccessible by heroes, to travel to the other pit. How he dug this cave system and gets to the other side so quickly will remain a trade secret.

Roshan does not drop Aghanim's Blessing upon his third death on the radiant side anymore. Roshan now drops Tome of Aghanim, an item exclusively given only by him in the game, which grants the user hero their Aghanim's Scepter upgrade for 5 minutes.

Jungle Creeps gold and experience rewards are now dynamic : Their bounties are 40% more at the start of the game, but they decrease by 10% every 5 minutes for 20 minutes. After that their bounties increase by 10% every 5 minutes for 20 minutes and repeat.

Night time movement speed buff on units and heroes has been removed.

Denied Creeps now give 25% of their gold bounty to the denier.
Lane Creeps had their levels decreased :

Melee Creeps level are decreased from 3 to 2
Ranged Creeps level are decreased from 5 to 4
Ancient Creeps had their levels increased:

Small Ancient Creeps levels are increased from 3 to 10.

Big Ancient Creeps levels are increased from 6 to 12.
Towers attack range are increased from 700 to 800.

Tier 2 towers do not have Split Shot ability anymore.

Tower Protection aura is now only applied around tier 3/4 towers, furthermore it only grants bonus armor now instead of bonus health regeneration too.

Tower Protection aura bonus armor decreased from +3/+5/+5/+5 to +3.

Flagbearer Creeps now only spawn on the mid lane and grants 175% of the experience of a melee creep instead of bonus gold.

Flagbearer Creep Inspirational Aura now gives +10% movement speed in a 600 radius instead of +3 bonus health regeneration.

There is now an extra melee creep that spawns on both teams offlane.

Lotus Pools now generate Healing Lotuses every 2 minutes, instead of every 3 minutes.

Bounty Runes reworked:

They now spawn at minute 2, near the ancient camp, and continue to spawn every 2 minutes. Their bounty, which is 90 gold and experience, is no longer granted to the whole team; instead, it is given only to the hero that picks them up. This bounty scales as the game progresses, granting +15 gold and experience with each spawn.
Wisdom Runes have been removed.

Spell Immunity and Debuff Immunity have been reworked:

Debuff Immunity now only grants complete resistance against negative effects, but the abilities still do full damage to units in this state, and previously acquired debuffs will not be dispelled, only temporarily ineffective.
Spell Immunity now grants 100% spell damage resistance and makes units in this state untargetable by most spells.
Spell Lifesteal reduction on creeps reduced from 75% to 50%.

New state: Frozen. Frozen units are forbidden from casting spells and attacking but can use items. Abilities that now use this mechanic are, for example, Crystal Maiden's Frostbite and Drow Ranger's Frost Arrows. This state is dispelled by any kind of forced movement, for example Force Staff's Force ability.

Break is now always dispellable and does not get applied on abilities that are in the ultimate ability slot.

Hex now has two versions:

Transforming a unit into a pig now also removes the unit's secondary attributes, for example, magic resistance, evasion, and spell lifesteal. However, these units now move faster than before; their movement speed has been increased from 140 to 300, and their movement speed can still be increased via spells like Surge.
Units transformed into a frog keep their secondary stats but move even slower than before; their movement speed has been decreased from 140 to 100, and their movement speed cannot be increased anymore.

Roots now get dispelled by any kind of forced movement, for example Force Staff's Force ability. (Heroes like Slark still can not use Pounce while rooted and unroot themselves.)

Units with the flying state now elevate from the ground by 190 units, therefore heroes with a shorter attack range than 190 cannot attack them anymore.
Damage block is now a more prominent feature in the game, with strength heroes gaining it per strength as their class specific bonus and more items integrating it overall. The universal proc chance of most damage block giving items and abilities is 50%, unless stated otherwise.
BASE HERO CHANGES
Heroes now have their own innate abilities. These abilities differ from being a useful tool early on to being a game-changing late-game ability to abuse. They also differ in leveling: some can be leveled, while others cannot. A few of these innate abilities will be familiar, for example, Invoker's Invoke or Earth Spirit's Stone Remnants; however, others, like Chaotic Luck or Black Grimoire, will probably not be.

Heroes do not get a free Teleport Scroll anymore upon death.

Heroes do not have a base 25% magic resistance anymore; instead, they have a base 20% spell damage resistance

Innate damage block for melee heroes is removed.

There are now class specific benefits that heroes gain depending on their primary attribute :

Strength heroes now gain 0.5 damage block per strength, which works for ranged heroes too.

Agility heroes now gain 0.08% movement speed per agility.

Intelligence heroes now gain 0.06% cooldown reduction per intelligence.
DAMAGE TYPE CHANGES
Pure damage :

Does not get reduced by spell damage resistance or reductions but also does not benefit from spell amplification anymore.

Chaos damage type :

A new type of magic damage that ignores magic resistance, but gets reduced by spell damage reductions and resistance.
Controlled damage type :

Creeps set under control by Helm of The Dominator or a summon hero like Chen now gain this damage type. Units with this damage type deal 200% damage to heroes, 150% to creeps and 100% to structures.
