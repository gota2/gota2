JUNGLE UPDATES
JUNGLE ZONE AURAS
REWORKED
Well Wishes, Mighty Mines, Stalwart Statues, and Skeleton Strength map auras have been removed.
 
Heroes and their units now gain 2 bonus health regeneration and 1 bonus mana regeneration when traversing through the center of their own native jungle, while they gain 25 bonus movement speed when traversing through the enemy forests.
 
MISCELLANEOUS
Neutral Camps were relocated, and new paths have been added to discover while navigating inside the forests.

Rune UPDATES
RUNE SYSTEM
REWORKED
There are now 3 categories that each Rune belongs to:
 
Offensive Power Runes
Defensive Power Runes
Utility Runes
 
OFFENSIVE & DEFENSIVE POWER RUNES
Offensive Power Runes and Defensive Power Runes spawn at the previous power Rune spots, on both sides of the river every four minutes, starting at minute 4.
 
Both Offensive and Defensive Power Runes spawn at this interval, and if an Offensive Power Rune spawns on the top-side, then a Defensive Power Rune will spawn on the bot-side.
 
Offensive and Defensive Power Runes refill three Bottle charges.
 
List of Offensive Power Runes:
 
Double Damage Rune
Magic Damage Amplification Rune NEW  
 
List of Defensive Power Runes:
 
Armor Rune NEW  
Magic Resistance Rune NEW  
 
UTILITY RUNES
Utility Runes spawn in the main jungle of both sides every two minutes, starting at minute 2.
 
The first Utility Rune that spawns after the horn, and every third will always be a Bounty Rune.
 
Utility Runes only refill two Bottle charges.
 
List of Utility Runes:
 
Bounty Rune
Illusion Rune
Regeneration Rune
Haste Rune
Arcane Rune

Aghanim's Shard UPDATES
AGHANIM'S SHARDS
REWORKED
Aghanim's Shard is no longer purchaseable. It now drops from killing Roshan as a reward.
 
Aghanim's Shard no longer grants a new ability nor improves an already existing one. Instead, it now can be used to replace a component when building Aghanim's Scepter.
CREEP UPDATES
NEUTRAL CREEP SCALING
REWORKED
Neutral Creeps now gain a level every 4 minutes. When they level up, they gain 4 bonus attack damage and 0.8 bonus armor. Their levels can be viewed at the same location as hero levels.
 
When a Neutral Creep is dominated, it instead levels up every time its owner levels up.
 
ANCIENT CREEPS
Ancient Creeps now have 100% Magic Resistance.
Small Ancient Creep Levels increased from 3 to 8.
Big Ancient Creep Levels increased from 6 to 10.
 
MEGA CREEPS
Mega Creeps now spawn with 4 bonus damage and 0.8 bonus armor per consecutive wave.
 
FLAGBEARER CREEPS
REWORKED
Flagbearer Creeps now only spawn on the mid-lane and grant 200% of the experience of a melee creep when killed.
 
Inspirational Aura now grants 15% bonus movement speed in a 1200-radius to allied units instead of 3 bonus health regeneration.
 
MISCELLANEOUS
Lane Creeps no longer gain 15 bonus movement speed during night-time.
Creep Experience Penalty when denied increased from 50% to 75%.

ROSHAN ATTRIBUTES
Roshan now has Debuff Immunity.
Roshan Base Health increased from 6000 to 9000
Roshan Base Health Regeneration increased from 20 to 30
 
ROSHAN REWARDS
REWORKED
Killing Roshan now upgrades the next three waves to Mega Creep waves with catapults.
 
Roshan now drops the same rewards on both sides:
 
First Death: Aegis of the Immortal + Aghanim's Shard
Second Death: Aegis of the Immortal + Slice of CheeseConsume this delicious treat to gain 1250 health and 750 mana. + 2X Aghanim's Shards

HERO CLASS UPDATES
TALENT SYSTEM
REMOVED
Talents have been removed from the game. Explained in detail in the "Graveyard" section on the Main Page.
 
HERO CLASS PERKS
NEW
Heroes now gain class specific benefits depending on their primary attribute.
 
Strength heroes now gain 0.35 damage block per strength.The chance to block is 50% and works on ranged heroes too.
 
 
Agility heroes now gain 0.07% movement speed per agility.
 
Intelligence heroes now gain 0.06% cooldown reduction per intelligence.
 
INNATE ABILITIES
NEW
Every Hero in the game now has an innate ability.
 
These passive and active abilities differ from being a useful tool early on to being a game-changing late-game ability to abuse. They also differ in leveling: some can be leveled, while others cannot. A few of these innate abilities will be familiar, for example, Invoker's Invoke or Earth Spirit's Stone Remnants; however, others, like Chaotic Luck or Black Grimoire, will probably not be.
 
FLUID HEROES
NEW
New Hero Class. These heroes allow the players to build them however they want, depending on their playstyle, by having a fluid primary attribute. The primary attribute is determined by which attribute the hero has the most of. For example, a hero like Pangolier, who gains more agility than strength, can be turned into a strength hero by buying items that give strength.
 
Fluid Heroes gain the specific hero class perk according to their primary attribute at the time.
 
UNIVERSAL HEROES
REWORKED
Hero Pool reduced from 34 to 8. These heroes are now Void Spirit, Enigma, Faceless Void, Chaos Knight, Keeper of the Light, IO, Elder Titan, and Weaver.
 
Universal Heroes now fully benefit from every attribute's bonus perks, such as damage block per strength, movement speed per agility, and cooldown reduction per intelligence.
 
Universal Hero damage gain per stat reduced from 0.7 to 0.5.
 
CREEP-HEROES
REWORKED
Creep-Heroes are now even more similar to heroes. They are now treated by every ability in the game as heroes, and are capable of leveling up. They level-up when their owner levels up and gain 4 bonus attack damage and 0.8 bonus armor with each level.
 
Heroes and summons affected by this: Brewmaster (Elemental Spirits), Lone Druid (Spirit BearSpirit Bear already had a similar scaling rule.
 
), Visage (Familiars), and Warlock (Chaotic Golem).
 
MISCELLANEOUS
Heroes no longer gain 30 bonus movement speed during night-time.
Heroes no longer get a free Teleport Scroll upon death.
Melee Heroes no longer have Innate Damage Block.
Heroes Maximum Level decreased from 30 to 25
 
HERO KILL UPDATES
HERO KILL GOLD FORMULA
REWORKED
OLD   HeroBounty = 125 + (DeadHeroLevel x 8) + StreakGold
 
NEW   HeroBounty = 2 x [ 2 x (DeadHeroLevel + 0.01 x DeadHeroNetworth)] + StreakGold
 
AOE ASSIST GOLD FORMULA
REWORKED
OLD   AssistGold = (30 + VictimNetWorth x 0.038) x NetWorthDifferenceFactor / NumberOfHeroes
 
NEW   AssistGold = [(HeroBounty - StreakGold) / 2] / NumberOfHeroes
 
FIRST BLOOD
First Blood now also grants 100 bonus experience.
 
STRUCTURE UPDATES
MOMENTUM SURGE
NEW
New Buff. Structures upon destruction now grant this buff to enemy units within 1200-radius. Grants 10% bonus movement speed and 40 bonus attack speed for 10 seconds.
 
GLYPH OF FORTIFICATION
REWORKED
No longer grants Multishot ability during the duration.
No longer grants invincibility to allied creeps during the duration.
 
Now grants 100% damage reduction to allied structures for 20 seconds. The damage reduction gradually decreases over the duration.
 
TWIN GATES
REWORKED
Twin Gates were moved to the location where the Tormentors used to be.
 
Twin Gates do not require to be channeled anymore; the teleporting is instant; however, they place a dispellable debuff on the teleported units that silences and mutes them for 4 seconds. Twin Gates cannot be used while the user is silenced, preventing instant teleports back.
 
LOTUS POOLS
REWORKED
Lotus Pools were moved to the center of the primary jungles.
 
Lotus Pools now spawn a Healing Lotus every 2 minutes on the ground around the structure, ready to be picked up.
 
Lotus Pools can now be channeled to instantly restore 50 health and 100 mana. Once channeled, they dry up and go on cooldown for 3 minutes. While it is on cooldown, the Healing Lotus spawning is delayed and temporarily stopped. If the channeler has a Bottle, then it will be filled with a Water Rune.

MECHANIC UPDATES
PSEUDO-CHANNEL
NEW
New Mechanic. Pseudo-Channel is similar to a normal channel but allows movement while channeling with some penalty.
 
PARALYZE
NEW
New Mechanic. Paralyze applies a 100% movement speed slow, 100% attack speed reduction, and a 100% cast speed slow for some duration.
 
ATTACK SPEED REDUCTION IMMUNITY
NEW
New Mechanic. Attack Speed Reduction Immunity prevents attack speed slows from taking effect.
 
MAGIC LIFESTEAL
REWORKED
Spell Lifesteal has been replaced with Magic Lifesteal. Magic Lifesteal does not restore health from pure and physical ability damage.
 
Magic Lifesteal now has a 400-range buffer. When dealing magic damage outside of this range, it no longer restores health.
 
LIFESTEAL
REWORKED
Lifesteal no longer has a creep penalty.
 
Lifesteal now has a 400-range buffer. When dealing attack damage outside of this range, it no longer restores health.
 
HEX
REWORKED
Hex now has two types:
 
Basic Hex. Transforms a unit into a frog, applying silence, mute, disarm, and a 50% movement speed slow for the duration.
 
Strong Hex. Transforms a unit into a sheep, applying silence, mute, disarm, break, and a 75% movement speed slow for the duration.
 
ROOT
REWORKED
Root now has two types:
 
Basic Root. Prevents the target from moving, casting mobility abilities, and grants True Sight over it for the duration.
 
Strong Root. Prevents the target from moving, attacking, casting abilities, and grants True Sight over it for the duration.
 
Root effects are now removed when the target is forcefully moved.Examples of forced movement: Force (Force Staff), Dispose (Marci), Meat Hook (Pudge).
 
 
MAGIC AMPLIFICATION
Spell Amplification has been replaced with Magic Amplification. Magic Amplification does not increase pure and physical ability damage.
 
UPHILL MISS CHANCE
Uphill Miss Chance is now dependent on the level difference between the two units, allowing a maximum of 75% miss chance when there is a three-level difference.
 
BREAK
Break no longer affects Ultimate-type abilities.
 
BANISH & HIDE
Banish/Hide now prevents the affected units from regenerating health and mana.
 
BACKPACK
Backpack Items are no longer visible to enemy units.
 
AURA EFFECTS
Aura Effects no longer have a linger duration.
 
EXPERIENCE
Experience Acquisition Range decreased from 1500 to 1200

RENAMED

DEBUFF RESISTANCE
RENAMED
Status Resistance has been renamed to Debuff Resistance.
DEBUFF IMMUNITY UPDATES
DEBUFF IMMUNITY
REWORKED
Debuff Immunity no longer grants Magic Resistance, Pure Damage Immunity, and Reflected Damage Immunity.
 
Debuff Immunity is now the same mechanic as Debuff Resistance.Status Resistance has been renamed to Debuff Resistance.
 
 It is equal to 100% Debuff Resistance. When an ability is cast on you while you are affected by Debuff Immunity, the ability will still deal damage, but the debuff simply expires without ever affecting you. This also means that Debuff Immunity piercing abilities now also pierce Debuff Resistance.
 
MAGIC IMMUNITY
NEW
New State. Magic Immunity is Debuff Immunity combined with 100% Magic Resistance. In this state, you are immune to magical damage and debuffs. Note that you still take damage from abilities with physical, pure, and reflected damage type.
