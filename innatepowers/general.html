REWORKED
Aghanim's Shard is no longer a purchasable item from the shop; instead, it now drops from killing Roshan as a reward.

CREEP UPDATES
NEUTRAL CREEP SCALING
REWORKED
Neutral Creeps now gain a level every 4 minutes. When they level up, they gain +4 Attack Damage and +0.8 Armor. Their levels can be viewed at the same location as hero levels.
When a Neutral Creep is dominated, it instead levels up every time its owner levels up.

Ancient Creeps now have 100% Magic Resistance.
Small Ancient Creep Levels increased from 3 to 8.
Big Ancient Creep Levels increased from 6 to 10.

Mega Creeps now spawn with +4 Attack Damage and +0.8 Armor per consecutive wave.
FLAGBEARER CREEPS
REWORKED
Flagbearer Creeps now only spawn on the mid-lane and grant 200% of the experience of a melee creep when killed.

HERO CLASS UPDATES
TALENT SYSTEM
REMOVED
Talents have been removed from the game. Explained in detail in the "Graveyard" section on the Main Page.
HERO CLASS PERKS
NEW
Heroes now gain class specific benefits depending on their primary attribute.
Strength heroes now gain +0.1% Magic Resistance per Strength.
Agility heroes now gain +0.07% Movement Speed per Agility.
Intelligence heroes now gain +0.1% Magic Amplification per Intelligence.
INNATE ABILITIES
NEW
Every Hero in the game now has an innate ability.
These passive and active abilities differ from being a useful tool early on to being a game-changing late-game ability to abuse. A few of these innate abilities will be familiar, for example, Invoker's Invoke or Earth Spirit's Stone Remnants; however, others, like Chaotic Luck or Black Grimoire, will probably not be.

Twin Gates do not require to be channeled anymore; the teleporting is instant; however, they place a dispellable debuff on the teleported units that silences and mutes them for 4 seconds. Twin Gates cannot be used while the user is silenced, preventing instant teleports back.

REWORKED

Lotus Pools can now be channeled to instantly restore 50 health and 100 mana. Once channeled, they dry up and go on cooldown for 3 minutes. While it is on cooldown, the Healing Lotus spawning is delayed and temporarily stopped. If the channeler has a Bottle, then it will be filled with a Water Rune.

DEBUFF IMMUNITY UPDATES
DEBUFF IMMUNITY
REWORKED
Debuff Immunity no longer grants Magic Resistance, Pure Damage Immunity, and Reflected Damage Immunity.
Debuff Immunity is now the same mechanic as Debuff Resistance/Status Resistance. It is equal to 100% Debuff Resistance. When an ability is cast on you while you are affected by Debuff Immunity, the ability will still deal damage, but the debuff simply expires without ever affecting you.
Abilities can no longer piece Debuff Immunity.
ABILITY UPDATES
DISABLE TYPE
NEW
New Ability Property. Abilities now have a new property, replacing the arbitrary rules regarding Debuff Immunity piercing: Disable Type. Every disable in the game - stun, root, banish, or taunt - is now either a Soft Disable or a Hard Disable.
Soft Disable: These abilities work how previous disables worked; affected by status effect manipulating sources; whether this meant increasing or decreasing the disable duration.
Hard Disable: These abilities have an absolute duration that cannot be increased or decreased; they are unaffected by status effect manipulating sources.
